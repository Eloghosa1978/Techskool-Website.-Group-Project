FOR KIDS AND TEENS

Prepare Them For The Future

Tech Skool helps kids and teens develop in-demand Technology and Business skills from the basics to applied learning outcomes. We offer applicable real-world courses, and through junior internships, we provide entry-level to professional-level work experience for our learners, positioning them for a winning future.

Who Can Enroll

Admissions are open for Kids and Teens within 7-17 Years. If the live class schedules are favorable, you can join from any part of the world.

Learning Schedule

Learning is self-paced & monitored. Learners get video-based lessons during weekdays and a compulsory Live class on Saturdays.

Classroom Style

We designed a safe and interactive online classroom and campus for our learners to explore, learn, and collaborate from anywhere.
