FOR KIDS AND TEENS

Prepare Them For The Future

Tech Skool helps kids and teens develop in-demand Technology and Business skills from the basics to applied learning outcomes. We offer applicable real-world courses, and through junior internships, we provide entry-level to professional-level work experience for our learners, positioning them for a winning future.

Who Can Enroll

Admissions are open for Kids and Teens within 7-17 Years. If the live class schedules are favorable, you can join from any part of the world.

Learning Schedule

Learning is self-paced & monitored. Learners get video-based lessons during weekdays and a compulsory Live class on Saturdays.

Classroom Style

We designed a safe and interactive online classroom and campus for our learners to explore, learn, and collaborate from anywhere.

Curriculum & Approach

At Tech Skool, we understand that everyone has different interests and needs. That’s why we strive to provide a diverse selection of Tech Courses that are tailored to specific interests. However, our Business Design and Entrepreneurship Course is a required course for all learners, as it covers general topics.

Our courses for kids and teens follow a termly model and are offered as a co-curricular activity to complement the existing academic curriculum provided by their traditional schools. This approach ensures that neither the learners nor their parents feel overburdened by the classroom schedule.

Our courses are accelerated and can be completed in one learning session and at the end of the course the learners undergo a Junior Internship, competition, and project showcase to consummate the course.
