ABOUT TECH SKOOL EDUCATION
Tech Skool Educational Institution is an institute of Technology and Entrepreneurship education designed to provide wholesome learning experiences for learners of different age groups, providing entry-level to professional-level education and experience in Technology and entrepreneurship skills.
MISSION

To deliver sustainable technology and entrepreneurship education, positioning the learners for global economic impact.

VISION

To Raise the next generation of tech talents, leaders, and industry captains, for Africa and beyond.

APPROACH

Innovation and entrepreneurship, and learning outcomes are the drivers and key words, hence our very unique strategies.

Company Brief

Tech Skool Education was founded in June 2022 as a member of E Skool Now Group by Joshua Ogbonnia. We have put intentional structures and processes in place to deliver excellence to our learners with desired outcomes, which are transformed societies.
